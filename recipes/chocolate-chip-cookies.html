Chocolate Chip Cookies From AllRecipes

Is it Christmas Eve? Is Santa on his way? These chocolate chip cookies will leave Santa wanting more.

Full of gooey melted chocolate, these chocolate chip cookies are a favorite at any party!

Ingredients

  • Butter:Be sure to get the butter out a few hours before beginning to bake for a perfect blend.
  • Eggs:Eggs add the moisture and hold the dough together! Another ingredient you will want to get out a few hours before baking.
  • Vanilla:Vanilla extract enhances the overall flavor. Extra points if it's homemade.
  • Baking soda:Baking soda acts as a leavener and helps the cookies rise.
  • Water:A bit of hot water creates steam as it bakes which makes the cookies puffy.
  • Salt:A pinch of salt enhances the flavors of the other ingredients. Don't worry, your cookies won't taste salty!
  • Flour:All-purpose flour helps create gluten, which adds structure to the cookie dough.
  • Chocolate chips:Of course, don't forget the most important ingredient! Use whichever flavor you prefer.

Steps

  1. Beat the butter and sugars, then beat in the eggs and vanilla.
  2. Dissolve the baking soda in hot water and add to the mixture.
  3. Stir in the flour, chocolate chips, and walnuts.
  4. Drop dough onto a prepared baking sheet.
  5. Bake until the edges are golden brown (usually about ten minutes).
