Birria de Res Tacos from AllRecipes

Birria is a Mexican plate of stewed braised meat. Done correctly, birria should feel as if it is melting in your mouth. This flavorful dish is great for any occassion.

Birria is best served with a full plate of rice, refried beans, and pineapple salsa. If paired with a margarita, your friends will beg to have dinner at your place more often.

Ingredients

  • Dried chile peppers:This will add a kick to your tasty dinner if you don't remove all the seeds.
  • BeefBeef chuck roast is perfect for that pull apart melt in your mouth feeling that you are going for.
  • SeasoningsSalt, pepper, fresh garlic, whole cloves, cinnamon, cumin, thyme, marjoram, and garlic should get you started.
  • Oilive oilSear the meat in olive oil before adding the braised liquid.
  • TomatoesGrilled roma tomatoes will unlock bold flavors when blending them with other ingredients
  • VinegarTwo tbsp of white vinegar adds the perfect cut of acidity to the braising liquid.
  • TortillasUsing store-bought corn tortillas will work, but nothing kicks as much as a homemade corn tortilla.
  • Onionand cilantroFinishing is the most important part!
