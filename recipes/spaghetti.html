Baked Spaghetti Recipe from AllRecipe

Spaghetti is a classic childhood throwback for my family. To switch things up, I sometimes take a family tradition and twist it a bit making baked spaghetti.

Baked spaghetti can be a good meal for when things are rushed but you still want to put a warm dinner on the table for two or more.

Ingredients

  • Noodles:Spaghetti noodles work best for...spaghetti.
  • Beef and onion:The meat sauce begins with a mixture of ground beef and diced onion.
  • Sauce:A jar of meatless spaghetti sauce can act as a base to your sauce,though you can substitute this for homemade or other sauces if desired.
  • Salt:Seasoned salt enhances the overall flavor.
  • Eggs:Eggs moisturize and help hold the baked spaghetti together.
  • Cheeses:You'll need Parmesan, mozzerella, and cottage cheese to start. Whatever we recommend, you could probably double.
  • Butter:Melted butter gives the dish extra richness and delight.

Steps

  1. Boil and drain the spaghetti.
  2. Cook the beef and onion together, then drain off the excess oil.
  3. Add the sauce and salt. Whisk the eggs, Parmesan, and butter in a separate bowl.
  4. Toss the spaghetti in the Parmesan mixture.
  5. Layer the ingredients in a prepared baking dish according to the details of the recipe.
  6. Cover and bake for 40 minutes. Sprinkle with mozzarella, then keep baking until the cheese is melted.
